Ordering & delivery

  • How does Clezer work?

    Clezer allows you to share payments as a group in real time, using just one Clezer payment card.

    Securely create groups, define payments limits using the Clezer app, and spend online or instore with your Clezer card that is connected to he group.

    You can select the group that you would like to pay from by simply opening the Clezer app and tapping the group card - that group will instantly become "active", ready for you to spend with as a group.

    Whenever you spend with Clezer in-store, online, or ATMs, we'll charge the group that you have selected in the app (this is done in seconds while you're at the till!) - you can check your spend history and your currently active card in the app anytime.

  • What is Clezer?

    Clezer simplifies your group expenses life by connecting your friends to one smart card and an even smarter app.

    Load your Mastercard® and Visa® debit or credit cards into the Clezer app, and spend/participate to your froiends group expenses using just one Clezer card.

    Save when you & your friends spend abroad

    When using your bank cards abroad, most banks add up to 5% in fees for currency exchange. Eliminate those fees with Clezer and get access to the market rate for currency exchange +1%.
    No hidden fees and no top-up. The only card you need to pack.

    Intelligent fraud protection

    All group participant get an alert every time the group card is used. The group addmin(creator) can instantly lock the card in the app if he sees a fraudulent payment. As a participant, you can opt-out anytime from any group you participate in, the other participants will be notified in reall time for every change/update made to the group. Now ask yourself: can your bank cards do that?

    A bookkeeper for your group in your pocket

    Clezer allow you to share payments in real time along the way of your social interaction and shared moments with your friends. No need to remember who paid what and how owns who money and how much... Just get the most of your time together.

  • Who can get Clezer?

    Clezer is currently available for users in France, the UK and Spain.

    If you're not eligible at the moment, sign up to our waiting list for when we launch our consumer card in early January 2018.

  • How do I get a Clezer?

    You can intantly generate a Clezer card by downloading the Clezer app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

    Once you’ve downloaded the app, follow the sign-up instructions and get set up in minutes - if you order a physical card, we’ll send you a confirmation email to let you know that your order is complete.

    If you’ve been given a card in person, you simply need to activate it by downloading the Clezer app and tapping ‘I was given a Clezer card’.

  • How much does Clezer cards cost?

    Clezer is an on-demand prepaid payment card designed for group payments.

    Your first 2 digital cards are free and can accomodates up to 4 participants for each payments. Each additional digital card cost 6€ split accross the group participant, with unlimited number of participants
