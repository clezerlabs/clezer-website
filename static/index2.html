How is CLEZER card different?

We bring group payments to a whole new level by introducing features that make split payments simple, fair, and secure.

Real Time

All transactions are split instantly. Not before, not after... just right on time! All participant get notified in real time as the payment happen.

Accurate

Only the exact amount is debited from participant account. Not more, not less... just what is required!

Universal

CLEZER card is a payment card that is accepted everywhere. No constraint, no restriction...just simple!

How does it work?
3 simple steps...

Create a group.

Just like you would do on Whatsapp or by email, create a group payment from the Clezer app, and start chatting about your next plans.

You and your friends can discuss your next ski holidays, a bachelor’s party weekend, New Year’s Eve, a trip to the 2018 World Cup or just a common gift…

Manage card limit.

Each group participant defines his or her individual limit for the payment from the CLEZER app.

At this point, there is no money transfer yet; just a pre-approval from the participants. The CLEZER card limit will be set to the sum of the limits defined by each participant.

Create your CLEZER card.

Instantly generate a Virtual card for any online or in-store payments.

You can then start making payments on behalf of your group. Each card transaction will be fairly* split instantly using the individual accounts connected to the card.
